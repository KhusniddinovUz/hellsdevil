My adventure in the world of competitive gaming began in 2016 when I became a professional player in Vainglory. Those early days were intense, full of hard work, and they laid the foundation for my career in esports. Competing at a professional level taught me the importance of strategy, adaptability, and the teamwork needed to excel in any high-stakes environment.

As mobile esports evolved, so did my career. In 2021, I stepped into an analyst role during a major Wild Rift tournament in Europe. This was an incredible opportunity to dive deep into game strategy and player analysis, giving me a new perspective on the game and a chance to contribute to the competitive scene in a fresh way. Analyzing top-level play was exciting, and I felt a renewed drive to push my career further.

By 2022, I took on an additional challenge - casting. What started as a new venture quickly turned into a successful role that allowed me to connect with the audience and bring the game’s most intense moments to life. My passion for casting didn’t go unnoticed, and soon I was invited to join the casting team for Wild Rift Worlds. Bringing my game knowledge and enthusiasm to the world stage, while sharing the thrill of the competition, was an unforgettable experience.

From pro player to analyst and caster, my journey in esports has been all about growth, dedication, and a love for the game. With each new role, I’ve gained a deeper understanding of competitive gaming and the skills it takes to succeed at the highest levels. I’m excited to keep contributing to the esports world, helping others see the depth and excitement that make these games so captivating.
